# Umarise — Two-Track Strategy

## Binding Strategic Framework

**Status:** Approved Strategy Document  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Applies to:** All Umarise development (Core + App)  
**Last updated:** February 2026

---

## Executive Summary

Umarise operates on two parallel tracks that share the same primitive but serve fundamentally different necessities, timelines, and audiences. These tracks must never be merged or confused.

| Track | Layer | Necessity | Timeline | Posture |
|-------|-------|-----------|----------|---------|
| **A** | Umarise Core | Juridical / External | 12–36 months | Silent |
| **B** | Umarise App | Epistemological / Cognitive | Now | Speaking |

---

## Track A: Umarise Core (B2B)

### The Juridical Necessity

> *"Without an externally anchored beginning, internal claims of first existence are not admissible."*

This is a **juridical and institutional truth**, not a persuasion.

### Core Principles

| Concept | Meaning |
|---------|---------|
| Proof ≠ Declaration | A claim is not evidence |
| Internal ≠ Admissible | Self-generated records fail external scrutiny |
| Beginning ≠ Reconstructable | Origins cannot be proven after the fact |

### Trigger Event

External parties (auditors, courts, regulators, counterparties) declare:

> *"We do not accept this."*

### Focus (Correct)

- External anchoring
- Write-once immutability
- Independence from creating party
- Meaning-agnostic (no interpretation)
- Non-negotiable constraints

### Timeline

⏳ **12–36 months** (unpredictable, but inevitable)

### Strategic Posture

> **Do not convince. Only wait.**

Core exists for the moment when internal claims are no longer accepted. Until that moment arrives organically, Core remains silent.

---

## Track B: Umarise App (B2C)

### The Epistemological Necessity

> *"Without a fixed starting point, no change can be objectively evaluated."*

This is **not a legal problem** — it is a **knowledge problem**.

### Core Principles

| Concept | Meaning |
|---------|---------|
| Optimization without reference = Narrative | Claims of improvement require a baseline |
| Improvement without beginning = Feeling | Without origin, progress is subjective |
| Iteration without starting point = Drift | Change without anchor leads to loss |

### Trigger Event

People realize:

> *"We're changing everything, but we no longer know what the effect is."*

### Focus (Correct)

- Insight and awareness
- Reflection on beginnings
- Meaning and personal context
- Team and individual understanding
- "What was the beginning?"

### Timeline

⚡ **Now** (this wave has already begun)

### Strategic Posture

> **Explain, frame, and convince.**

The App must actively help people recognize why beginnings matter — before the external necessity arrives.

---

## The Critical Boundary (Non-Negotiable)

### The Trap

If the App ever **suggests that it provides the proof**, then:

- Core becomes blurred
- Umarise becomes "a product"
- Future inevitability is lost

### The Formula

> The App teaches you *why* the beginning matters.  
> Core exists for the moment when someone else demands proof.

### Binding Rule

```
The Umarise App may surface, contextualize, or encourage the recognition 
of beginnings, but it must never itself constitute external proof. 

All proof lives exclusively in Umarise Core.
```

---

## Role Distribution (Explicit)

### Umarise App (B2C / Epistemological)

**MAY:**
- Help people see what a beginning is
- Help choose which beginnings matter
- Help understand what is lost without anchoring
- Automatically compute a hash and forward to Core
- Provide personal insight and reflection tools

**MUST NOT:**
- Store proof
- Claim priority
- Function as a third party
- Suggest that app-level records constitute evidence

### Umarise Core (B2B / External)

**IS:**
- The only thing that counts as external beginning
- Hash + timestamp + origin_id
- Write-once and immutable
- Independent of app, user, and organization

**RULE:**
> If it's not in Core, it does not exist externally.

## The Song Example (Canonical)

### Scenario: A girl writes a song

**Without Umarise:**
1. Girl writes song
2. Saves it locally
3. Years later: another version becomes a hit
4. She claims: "I had this earlier"
5. Evidence: internal files, timestamps, declarations
6. ➡️ **Weak evidence** — reconstructable, not externally anchored

**With Umarise App (Correct Usage):**
1. Girl writes song
2. App does NOT say: "This is proof"
3. App says: *"This is a beginning that may be meaningful. Would you like to anchor it?"*
4. Optional: App triggers Core attestation (hash + time)
5. Core records: this hash existed at timestamp X
6. ➡️ Years later: she explains nothing, proves nothing via app
7. ➡️ She only **verifies via Core**

### Critical Distinction

| Layer | Role |
|-------|------|
| App | Recognizes the beginning |
| Core | Anchors the beginning |

The App is **never** the source of truth.

## Strategic Do's and Don'ts

### Do (Essential)

1. **Observe where internal beginnings already fail**
   - Lawsuits
   - Audits
   - AI provenance discussions

2. **Anchor language that disqualifies internal beginnings**
   - "self-attestation"
   - "post-hoc claims"
   - "internally generated evidence"

3. **Position Umarise as neutral consequence**
   - Not as solution, but as what remains when internal beginnings fail

### Don't (Forbidden)

1. ❌ Choose between A or B
2. ❌ Merge A and B
3. ❌ Let App claim proof status
4. ❌ Let Core explain or convince

### Do (Required)

1. ✅ Let A and B exist in parallel
2. ✅ Keep Core silent
3. ✅ Let App speak
4. ✅ Maintain the boundary
