# Umarise App — Experience Briefing

## Binding Design Specification

**Status:** Approved  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Applies to:** All Umarise App development  
**Last updated:** February 2026

---

## Intent (niet interpreteren, alleen uitvoeren)

> **The Umarise App exists to acknowledge a beginning — and then step aside.**

It does not explain the beginning.  
It does not improve it.  
It does not organize it.

It simply marks the moment someone says:

> *"This is where it began."*

---

## The Human Moment (core of the experience)

People live in systems that change faster than they can explain.

- Ideas evolve.
- Work accelerates.
- Creations are overwritten.
- Stories are retold.

In that world, **the beginning is fragile**.

The app exists for the single human impulse to pause and say:

> *"Before this changes — this mattered."*

**That pause is the product.**

---

## What the App Must Feel Like

| NOT this | BUT this |
|----------|----------|
| Productive | Grounding |
| Archival | Momentary |
| Social | Private |
| Impressive | Quiet |
| Expressive | Restrained |
| Useful after the moment | Complete in the moment |

It must feel like:

- **signing your name**, not publishing
- **acknowledging**, not presenting
- **relief**, not achievement
- **recognition without witnesses**

### Failure Conditions

- If it feels **impressive**, it is wrong.
- If it feels **expressive**, it is wrong.
- If it feels **useful after the moment**, it is wrong.

**The value is entirely in the act, not in what follows.**

---

## The One Allowed Action

The app allows exactly one meaningful action:

> **Mark this moment as a beginning.**

That action may:

- generate a fingerprint
- prepare an external anchor
- trigger Umarise Core

But the app itself **never claims proof, priority, or truth**.

It only creates the condition for proof elsewhere.

---

## Absolute Boundaries (Non-Negotiable)

The Umarise App **must never**:

| # | Boundary | Rationale |
|---|----------|-----------|
| 1 | Present itself as evidence | Proof lives in Core |
| 2 | Explain why a beginning is important | The moment explains itself |
| 3 | Rank, evaluate, or judge beginnings | All beginnings are equal |
| 4 | Turn beginnings into content | Not a publishing platform |
| 5 | Encourage revisiting, browsing, or optimizing | Not an archive |
| 6 | Create identity, feeds, collections, or timelines | Not social media |
| 7 | Ask the user to "do more" | The action is complete |

**If any of these appear, the app has failed.**

---

## Relationship to Umarise Core (Binding)

> The app explains why the beginning matters.  
> Umarise Core exists for the moment when explanation no longer suffices.

- The app may **initiate** anchoring.
- Only Core **constitutes** external, admissible proof.

**This separation must be felt — not explained.**

---

## Design North Star

> **The app should disappear the moment it succeeds.**

If a user closes it feeling:

- calmer
- more grounded
- less afraid of losing themselves to change

Then it worked.

**No retention goal overrides this.**

---

## Final Test (use this internally)

Ask only one question:

> *"Does this respect the beginning — or does it try to use it?"*

If it tries to use it, **remove it**.
